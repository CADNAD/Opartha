Language that is built by simple sounds for most common concepts which tendrils outwards for less and less common concepts.

SOUNDS

\-a (pronounced “ah”) 

\-e (pronounced “eh”)

\-y (pronounced “yea”)

\-é (pronounced “ee”)

\-I (pronounced “I”)

\-o (pronounced “oh”)

\-u (pronounced “you”)

\-ak (plural)

CONCEPTS

\-sa (subject/being)

\-sas (me)

\-san (you)

\-sa
